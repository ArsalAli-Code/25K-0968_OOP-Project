==========================================================
             FROSTBOUND: THE MELEE CHRONICLES
==========================================================

Welcome, Hero. You have entered the frozen realm where every 
swing of your blade costs energy and every mistake is fatal.

[ CHARACTER CLASSES ]
1. WARRIOR : Balanced stats. Good for beginners.
2. ROGUE   : High damage, but fragile. Requires stamina management.
3. TANK    : High health and defense. Can endure long battles.

[ COMBAT MECHANICS ]
* STAMINA (STM): Every attack consumes energy. 
  - Basic Attack   : 10 STM
  - Special Move   : 30 STM (2x Damage)
  - Ultimate Combo : 50 STM (3.5x Damage)
  
* EXHAUSTION: If your STM is too low, your attack will FAIL.
  Rest is vital! You recover 20 STM at the end of every turn.

* FREE ACTIONS: Using a Potion or changing gear from your 
  Inventory does NOT cost a turn. Use this to your advantage!

[ EQUIPMENT & ITEMS ]
* WEAPONS : Increase your base attack damage.
* ARMOR   : (Helmet, Chest, Legs, Boots) increase your Max HP.
* SHIELDS : Increase your Defense, reducing damage taken.
* POTIONS : Instantly restore HP or Stamina.

[ SAVING PROGRESS ]
The game tracks your Level, Gold, Current HP, and Inventory.
Progress is saved automatically when you return to the Hub,
or you can perform a Manual Save from the Camp Menu.
